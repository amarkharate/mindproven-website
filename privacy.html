Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2024

Manifest ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Manifest is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • User Preferences: Your selected categories, goals, and app settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Usage Data: Basic app usage statistics (such as affirmations viewed, streaks) are stored locally on your device.
  • Subscription Status: If you subscribe to Manifest: Daily Affirmations Premium, Apple handles all payment processing. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status.

How We Store Your Data

All your personal data (preferences, favorites, progress) is stored locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases and subscriptions. Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

Manifest is not intended for children under 17. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. Since data is stored locally, removing the app removes all associated data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
